1. Nasi Goreng

Nothing get's better than this. Nasi Goreng is an Indonesian fried rice dish typically served with a fried egg on top, flavored with soy sauce, shallots, and other seasonings.

2. Sate

Sate is a skewered meat dish usually chicken, covered in peanut sauce. The blend of sweet, salty, and umami is just to die for

3. Kue Cincin

A nice snack to end meals with or to drink with tea, not too sweet yet not too bland either
