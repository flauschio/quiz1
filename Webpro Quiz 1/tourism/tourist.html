1. Monas

Monas (National Monument) is an iconic symbol of Jakarta, representing the struggle for Indonesia's independence. Visitors can enjoy panoramic views of the city from its observation deck.

2. Museum Pahlawan

Museum Pahlawan is dedicated to honoring the heroes of Indonesia's independence. The museum showcases various artifacts and exhibitions related to the nation's history.

3. Taman Mini Indonesia Indah

Taman Mini Indonesia Indah is a cultural park that showcases the diverse cultures of Indonesia through traditional houses, museums, and exhibitions.
